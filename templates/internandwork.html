Internships and Work-Based Learning

Work-Based Learning (WBL) are opportunities that allow students to apply learning through practical experience that develops knowledge and skills necessary for success in careers and post-secondary education.

Guest Speakers, Industry Tours, Career Fairs, Job Shadowing, Informational Interviews, Internships, Work Experience, Integrated Projects, Student-Run Enterprises
